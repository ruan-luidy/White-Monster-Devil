Noodles With WhiteMonster

This is literally the pinnacle of gastronomy particularly MY FAVORITE MEAL OF MY FRICKING LIFE.

Things that you will need.

  • A INFAMOUS CAN OF WHITE MONSTER.
  • A KETTLE FOR BOILING WATER.
  • A CUPNOODLES.

ESTABLISHMENT OF THE DISH.

  1. GET THE KETTLE AND FILL UP WITH WATER FROM THE SINK...
  2. POUR THE WATER IN THAT CUPNOODLES BASTARD...
  3. AND FINALLY GET THAT WHITE MONSTER FROM YOUR REFRIGERATOR, FREEZING TO DEATH.
  4. GET IN YOUR SOFA AND BE PREPARED FOR THE BEST GASTRONOMY EXPERIENCE OF YOUR LIFE, (for something to watch i recommend some video of walking in kyoto with rainstorm.)
